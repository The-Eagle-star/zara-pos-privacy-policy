Last updated: June 11, 2026  ·  ZaraPOS

1 Introduction

Welcome to ZaraPOS.

ZaraPOS is a Point of Sale (POS) and business management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our application and related services.

By using ZaraPOS, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2 Information We Collect

Account Information

When creating an account, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Authentication credentials
  • Business profile information
Business Data

ZaraPOS allows businesses to manage operations and may collect:

  • Product and inventory records
  • Sales transactions
  • Purchase transactions
  • Supplier information
  • Customer information
  • Receipts and invoices
  • Employee information
  • Business reports and analytics
Customer Information

Information entered by businesses may include:

  • Customer names
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Purchase history
  • Loyalty information
Device and Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Device type
  • Device identifiers
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • IP address
  • Crash logs
  • Performance diagnostics
Analytics Data

We use analytics services to understand how ZaraPOS is used, including feature access, session duration, user engagement metrics, and error reports.

3 How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Process sales and transactions
  • Manage inventory and business operations
  • Generate reports and analytics
  • Improve application functionality
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4 Firebase Services

ZaraPOS uses services provided by Google Firebase, including:

Firebase Authentication

Used for secure user login and account management.

Cloud Firestore / Firebase Database

Used to securely store business, inventory, customer, supplier, and transaction data.

Firebase Analytics

Used to understand app usage and improve user experience.

Firebase Crashlytics

Used to monitor crashes and application performance issues.

Google may process certain technical information in accordance with its own Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy.

5 Data Storage

Business and user data is stored securely using Firebase cloud infrastructure.

Data may be stored and processed on servers located outside your country of residence. By using ZaraPOS, you consent to such storage and processing.

6 Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information only in the following situations:

  • With Firebase and Google Cloud services required to operate the application

  • To protect our legal rights and prevent fraud or security threats

7 Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, and destruction.

Despite our efforts, no internet-based system can guarantee absolute security.

8 Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide ZaraPOS services, maintain business records, and meet legal, accounting, and regulatory requirements.

Users may request account deletion subject to applicable legal obligations.

9 User Rights

Depending on applicable laws, users may have the right to:

  • Access their data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of data
  • Request data export
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

Requests may be submitted using the contact information below or through the Delete Account section on this page.

10 Children's Privacy

ZaraPOS is intended for business use and is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: June 11, 2026  ·  ZaraPOS

1 Request Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your ZaraPOS account and associated data at any time.

Email: mutesimugwaneza@gmail.com

Subject: Delete ZaraPOS Account

Include: The email address or phone number linked to your ZaraPOS account.

After receiving your request, we may contact you to verify account ownership before deleting your account and associated data.

2 Data That Will Be Deleted

When your account deletion request is verified, we will delete data associated with your ZaraPOS account, including:

  • User account information
  • Business profile information
  • Inventory and product records
  • Customer records
  • Supplier records
  • Sales transactions
  • Purchase records
  • Receipts and invoices
  • Employee records associated with the account
  • Application data linked to your account

3 Data That May Be Retained

Some information may be retained where necessary for legal, accounting, tax, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, security, or regulatory compliance purposes.

Backup copies may also remain in secure backup systems for a limited period before being automatically deleted.

4 Retention Period

Verified account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days of receipt.

Data stored in backups may be retained for up to 90 days before automatic deletion, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

5 Delete Some Data Without Deleting Your Account

Users may request deletion of certain data without deleting their ZaraPOS account, such as customer records, product records, supplier records, or transaction-related data, where legally allowed.

To request partial data deletion, email mutesimugwaneza@gmail.com with the subject Delete ZaraPOS Data and describe the specific data you want deleted.
